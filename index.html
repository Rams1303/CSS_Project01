Farming a better world
How we got here

The severe impoverishment of our agricultural biodiversity has been caused by intensive farming methods, genitically modified seeds and restraining farming regulations.

Practically all the fruits, vegetables and grains we consume today have been genetically modified.

our health
The standardization of our food supply has impoverished our nutrition and in turn has had an enormous impact on our health.
Our Current state

Today we need to eat potatoes to obtain the same nutrition content as a potatto grown in the 1940's.

Spinach today has 53% less potassium and 70% less phosphorus. Calcium are poorer in calcium by 50% and in magnesiumm by 75% (Halweil, 2007).

Our food stuff is now berft of most of its micronutirent content.

Seed Saving
Rehabilitating and saving the treasure off ancient and indegenous seeds has become humankind's sacred duty. (Rabhi & Duquesne, 2017)
